UPLIFT REGISTER

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Uplift Register collects and uses personal information submitted through its early-access, waitlist and launch communications pages.

1. Who we are

Uplift Register / The Uplift Register is a property intelligence and development opportunity platform.

Legal entity details are to be confirmed in the final version of this policy.

Privacy contact: privacy@upliftregister.ai

2. Information we collect

We may collect and process information such as:

  • name;
  • email address;
  • company or organisation details if submitted;
  • development geography or strategy preferences if submitted;
  • waitlist and early-access responses;
  • website usage and campaign attribution data; and
  • technical data such as IP address, browser or device data, and page interaction data where collected by analytics or advertising tools.

3. How we collect information

We may collect information through:

  • the early-access form;
  • the waitlist form and related embedded widgets;
  • LinkedIn Lead Gen forms;
  • website analytics, tracking tags and attribution tools; and
  • email replies or direct enquiries sent to Uplift Register.

4. How we use information

We may use collected information for purposes including:

  • managing early-access and waitlist requests;
  • assessing fit for controlled product release;
  • contacting applicants about access and launch updates;
  • improving launch communications and site performance;
  • measuring advertising and campaign performance;
  • preventing misuse, abuse or unauthorised use of the platform; and
  • preparing onboarding and subscriber operations where applicable.

5. Advertising and analytics

Uplift Register may use advertising, analytics and attribution tools in connection with its public launch pages.

This may include the LinkedIn Insight Tag, LinkedIn conversion tracking, campaign attribution systems, cookies or similar technologies, Cloudflare for traffic and security infrastructure, and GitHub Pages or related static-site delivery infrastructure for public-site hosting.

6. Sharing information

Personal information may be shared with limited service providers where reasonably necessary to operate the website, waitlist, analytics, advertising, email, hosting, security, onboarding or subscription-related flows.

Uplift Register does not sell personal information.

7. Legal basis

Depending on the context, Uplift Register may rely on consent where a user submits an early-access or waitlist request, legitimate interests in operating, securing and improving the service, and pre-contract steps where a user requests access to the product.

8. Data retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably needed for launch operations, early-access assessment, subscriber communications, product operations, legal or compliance purposes, and related record-keeping.

9. User rights

Subject to applicable law, users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal information.

10. Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, please contact: privacy@upliftregister.ai
